Hey there, I’m Barham — a UI Designer from Jordan.

I design responsive, user-friendly web and mobile interfaces that make people’s lives easier by turning complex interactions into simple, accessible experiences.

My journey into design started with a curiosity for technology and creativity.
What began as exploring how things work soon grew into a passion for crafting digital experiences that people actually enjoy using.

Today, I have over 2 years of experience as a UI Designer at ABC Programmers , where I focus on design systems, user flows, and building intuitive products in close collaboration with developers.
For me, design is more than arranging pixels on a screen, it’s about problem solving, storytelling, and making technology feel natural.

Outside of my professional work, I’m always exploring new sources of inspiration, from books and films to video games. These interests fuel my creativity and often spark fresh perspectives that I bring back into my design practice.
